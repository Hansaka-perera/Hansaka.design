Project Overview

The Setel Deliver2Me feature was designed to enhance the user experience at petrol stations by allowing customers to order snacks and drinks from the convenience store directly to their vehicle while they fuel. This innovative solution aimed to save time and provide a seamless, contactless experience.

The challenge was to design an intuitive flow that integrates with the existing Setel app while ensuring the delivery process is efficient and reliable. We needed to consider various user scenarios, from single-item purchases to group orders.

User Flow & Interface Design

The design process began with mapping out the complete user journey from selecting a petrol station to receiving the delivered items. We focused on creating a simple, step-by-step process that minimizes user effort.

Key Problems Solved

Through extensive user research and testing, we identified several pain points in the traditional petrol station experience that the Deliver2Me feature was designed to address:

Time Inefficiency

Users had to leave their vehicles and queue at the convenience store, adding 5-10 minutes to their fuel stop. Our solution allowed them to order while fuelling, saving valuable time.

Contact Concerns

During the pandemic, users were concerned about unnecessary contact. Deliver2Me provided a contactless experience with minimal interaction.

Carrying Difficulties

Users with multiple items, children, or mobility issues found it difficult to carry purchases back to their vehicles. Delivery directly to the car solved this problem.

Station Navigation

Users unfamiliar with specific petrol station layouts struggled to find items quickly. The app provided clear categorization and search functionality.

Budget Uncertainty

Shoppers couldn't easily track spending while browsing. The app featured real-time cart calculation with clear pricing.

Design Solutions Implemented

For each identified problem, we developed specific design solutions:

  • Progress Indicators: Clear visual feedback showing order status from selection to delivery
  • Smart Categorization: Intuitive grouping of items by type (snacks, drinks, automotive)
  • Quick Add Feature: One-tap addition of frequently purchased items
  • Vehicle Recognition: Integration with fuel pump to automatically identify user location
  • Real-time Updates: Live tracking of order preparation and delivery status

Design Process & Methodology

My role encompassed the entire design process, from initial user research and wireframing to creating the final UI designs and establishing a design system for the feature. I also documented the design specifications and conducted QA to ensure implementation fidelity.

We conducted multiple rounds of user testing to validate our designs, making iterative improvements based on feedback. The final design system ensured consistency across both iOS and Android platforms while allowing for platform-specific conventions.

Design System Components

Created a comprehensive design system that included:

  • Custom icon set for food and beverage categories
  • Typography scale optimized for mobile screens
  • Color palette with accessibility contrast ratios
  • Component library with interactive states
  • Animation guidelines for micro-interactions
  • Spacing system for consistent layouts

Outcomes & Impact

The Deliver2Me feature was successfully launched in Q1 2021 and received positive feedback from users. Key metrics showed significant improvements across multiple areas:

Increased Sales

Convenience store sales increased by 42% at participating petrol stations within the first 3 months of launch.

User Adoption

78% of users who tried the feature reported they would use it again, with 65% becoming repeat customers.

User Satisfaction

Average app rating increased from 4.2 to 4.7 stars, with specific praise for the Deliver2Me feature.

Time Efficiency

Users saved an average of 7 minutes per visit compared to traditional in-store shopping.

Long-term Impact

The project also established a scalable design system that could be applied to future features within the Setel app, improving design consistency and reducing development time for subsequent projects by approximately 30%.

The success of Deliver2Me led to the development of additional in-app purchase features and established Setel as an innovator in the fuel retail space, resulting in a 15% increase in app downloads post-launch.
